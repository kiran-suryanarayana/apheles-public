Questions

Pricing FAQ

Why is only the Starter plan available?

We're rolling out Apheles in phases. Starter covers Meta Ads and email — the channels where AI delivers the clearest, fastest results — and it's live now. Growth and Scale add more channels and intelligence as we ship them; we'd rather launch each one properly than half-build everything at once.

What happens when Growth and Scale launch?

Starter customers get early access to each new tier as it ships, and you can upgrade whenever you're ready. There's no obligation to move up — Starter remains a complete plan on its own.

Do I need marketing experience to use it?

No. Apheles is designed for the founder who handles their own marketing in the gaps of running a business. Every recommendation is explained in plain English, and you approve or reject with a tap.

Is my data safe, and do I stay in control?

Yes. You connect your own accounts via secure OAuth, and you can revoke access anytime. The AI only ever recommends — nothing goes live without your approval. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I cancel anytime?

Anytime, no contracts. You keep access through the end of your billing period.
