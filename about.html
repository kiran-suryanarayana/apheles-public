About Apheles

We think every small business deserves great marketing.

Apheles is a small, AI-first company building the marketing engine we wish small businesses had: powerful, honest, and simple enough to run in the gaps of a busy week.

Our story

Built for the people doing their own marketing.

Small businesses spend thousands a month on digital marketing and still feel in the dark. They stitch together six to ten tools, hire agencies they can't fully afford, and rarely get a straight answer about what's actually working.

We started Apheles because the technology to fix this finally exists. AI can now plan campaigns, write on-brand content, and read performance data well enough to make real recommendations — the work that used to require a whole marketing team.

So we built one platform that does it, end to end, and put the business owner in the driver's seat. The AI proposes; you decide. We even run Apheles on Apheles — our own ads, content, and email flow through the exact product we sell, so we feel every rough edge before our customers do.

What we believe

A few principles we won't compromise on.

01

The human stays in control

AI does the heavy lifting, but every recommendation is explained and waits for your approval. It's your brand, your budget, your call.

02

Honesty over hype

We report what worked and what didn't, in plain English. The numbers come from your real data — the AI never invents a result.

03

Simple beats clever

If a feature doesn't save you time or make you money, it doesn't ship. We'd rather do a few things brilliantly than everything adequately.

04

Priced for real businesses

Enterprise-grade marketing shouldn't require an enterprise budget. We build lean so we can keep it affordable.
